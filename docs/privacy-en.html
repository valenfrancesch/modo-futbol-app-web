Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Modo Futbol handles personal data when you use the mobile app and services at modofutbol.app.

1. Data controller

Contact: support@modofutbol.app

2. Data we collect

  • Account: email, name, nickname, avatar, preferred language, password (stored securely on the server).
  • Service use: predictions, group membership, news you publish if you are an admin.
  • Device: device identifier (device_id) and OS name sent at login for session management. This is not IDFA or IMEI: it is an app-generated UUID stored on the device until you uninstall the app or clear its data, and used on the server for that device’s session.
  • Ads: if ads are shown, Google AdMob may collect identifiers per Google's policy.

3. Purposes

  • Provide the prediction service.
  • Authentication and security.
  • Account-related emails (verification, password reset).
  • Display advertising in the free version when applicable.

4. Legal basis (EEA users)

Contract performance, legitimate interests (security, improvement), and consent where required (e.g. personalized ads via system or Google tools).

5. Retention

Data is kept while your account is active.

6. Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete account in the app.

When you confirm, your account is deleted on our systems (logical deletion): you will not be able to sign in again with the same credentials, because your email is anonymized and login will no longer work. We close your session and revoke associated access tokens.

Your predictions in groups may be kept in a way that no longer identifies you: standings are recalculated and you will no longer appear as a participant. News you published as an admin is marked as deleted according to service configuration.

7. Recipients

Cloud infrastructure and ad providers (Google). International transfers use appropriate safeguards when required.

8. Your rights

Access, rectification, erasure, objection, and complaint to your supervisory authority. Contact us or use Profile → Delete account in the app.

9. Children

Not directed at children under 13.

10. Changes

Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
