Child safety standards (CSAE)

Last updated: May 2026

Terms and Conditions · Privacy Policy · Help & support

These standards apply to the Modo Futbol App mobile application and related services operated at modofutbol.app. They describe our policies and procedures regarding child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

1. Zero tolerance

Modo Futbol App has zero tolerance for CSAE and CSAM. We prohibit any use of our services to create, upload, share, solicit, promote, or distribute content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors, or that facilitates such conduct.

2. Prohibited conduct and content

Users must not, through profiles, group news, messages, images, links, or any other feature:

  • Post, request, or distribute CSAM or content that sexualizes minors.
  • Groom, extort, or contact minors for sexual purposes.
  • Share or promote CSAE, including links to illegal material.
  • Use the service while impersonating a minor or encouraging minors to use the app without parental consent where required by law.
  • Harass or endanger children in any way.

Violations may result in immediate removal of content, suspension or termination of accounts, and reporting to competent authorities.

3. Scope of the Modo Futbol App service

Modo Futbol App is a football prediction pool app for private groups. It is not directed at children under 13. Features that may involve user-provided content include account profiles (name, nickname, avatar) and group news published by group administrators. We do not operate public open feeds or direct messaging between arbitrary users.

4. How to report concerns

If you see content or behavior in Modo Futbol App that may involve CSAE or CSAM, report it immediately:

  • In the app: use Profile (or the login screen) to open Help & support and contact us by email with a description of the issue, group name, and screenshots if possible.
  • By email: modo.futbol.app@gmail.com (subject: Child safety report).

If someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services first.

To report content on other platforms or websites, use the reporting tools of that service or contact your national hotline or law enforcement.

5. Our response to reports and CSAM

When we receive a credible report or otherwise obtain actual knowledge of CSAM or CSAE on Modo Futbol App, we will, as appropriate and without undue delay:

  • Investigate the report and preserve relevant information as required by law.
  • Remove or disable access to violating content and suspend or terminate responsible accounts.
  • Report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or to the relevant regional authority where we are legally required to do so.
  • Cooperate with law enforcement and with Google Play when notified of CSAE content linked to our app.

6. Legal compliance

We comply with applicable child safety laws and regulations in the jurisdictions where we operate. We review these standards periodically and update this page when our practices change.

7. Child safety point of contact

For notifications from Google Play or other partners regarding CSAE content in Modo Futbol App, contact our designated child safety point of contact:

modo.futbol.app@gmail.com

This contact can speak to our enforcement and review procedures and take action when required.
